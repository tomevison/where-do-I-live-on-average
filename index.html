Where do you live on average?

How It Works

Enter the cities or addresses you have lived in, along with the number of years spent at each location. The site calculates a weighted average location based on time spent at each place and shows it on the map. Everything is calculated in your browser – no addresses are stored or sent to our servers.

    Privacy Notice

    All calculations happen in your browser. We do not store or collect any location information. Your addresses never leave your device.
